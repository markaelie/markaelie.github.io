About Me


Hey there! My name is Mark Hunanyan (Markaelie). I am an aesthetic engineer.
I was born in 1991 at the tender age of zero. From an early age, I was accompanied by paintbrush and watercolor. After a few years, I was attending drawing classes. Everything seemed to be going well, but something was missing. After some years, when I got my first computer Dell Inspiron 7000 (this laptop was made in about 1997 or 1998 and was a low end laptop for the time) and was very familiar with 3D games, I still had the feeling of dissatisfaction in my heart. Equally, I was interested in the fact of how those games have been created. My interest in 3D graphics was growing more and more, simultaneously the quantity and quality of games were increasing. In 2004 I met Discreet 3dsmax 5. That time it seemed to be overwhelmingly difficult and I like that feeling when you know something complicated like creating 3d primitives with special parameters or boolean objects together. Slowly I learned to work with my own mistakes. Continuing my education in Educational Complex of Hakob Kojoyan (2004-2007), I kept on drawing which I have liked since childhood. In the final year of my studies, I came across the brochure of SAFAA which was concentrated on the sphere of CG. The Academy education was not so practical but fundamental (2007-2011). After graduating I got a job almost on the sphere of my specialization, working as a common digital graphic designer. With an experience increased during the years, I was approaching my specialization more and more and I started working in different places as Senior 3D Artist. Getting enough skills and practice of teaching, I started teaching CG as well (here). In 2014, I began to study programming, which allowed me to create projects like LUNETA, CODENAME PLAYA, and ProColor.
I got an experience of teamwork during years following the idea that all amazing projects are created due to the energy of passion.
